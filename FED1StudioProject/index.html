About Us

KC Rock Academy offers a unique and fun way to learn new instruments, and meet new people! Learn electric guitar, bass guitar, drums, and more from our renowned instructors who will help you to become the star you always knew you could become!

Why Choose Us

Student First

KC Rock Academy is all about the students. From one-on-one instruction, to full classes, each student gets an experience that's catered specifically towards them, in order to make sure they can succeed. Young or Old, Male or Female, and everyone in between, no one is excluded in our musical journey!

Options

KC Rock Academy offers both online and in-person lessons, so no matter where you are, you can learn at your leisure either at our 92nd Street location, or within the safety of your own home!
